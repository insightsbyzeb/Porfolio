Exploratory Data Analysis in Python

In this project, we go from data cleaning and pre-processing to exploratory data analysis using Python. The dataset explored is from a fictitious "Food Marketing Company". We segmented customers on various demographics (i.e., age and education) to gain new insights into the effectiveness of our marketing campaign on different customers. Results showed that targeting middle-aged customers with high income and no kids via different platforms would be the most effective for this company. (Libraries used include Pandas, Seaborn, and Matplotlib).
